Our Mission

Creating the next generation of technology leaders

We empower high school students with the resources and support they need to build thriving tech communities.

Our Story

CodeSpark Clubs was founded by a group of students in 2025 who recognized the challenges high school students face when trying to start tech clubs. We've simplified the process so you can focus on building a passionate community and exploring the exciting world of AI and computer science.

Birth of CodeSpark

Founded as an organization to help students start an AI club.

Expansion

CodeSpark Clubs launched as an online platform, offering AI club starter kits with guides and coding projects.

Growing Community

Our online resources reached multiple schools, inspiring students to launch their own AI and CS clubs.

Partnerships

We introduced new tools and guides to help students turn their ideas into reality through AI and CS clubs.

Today

Empowering student-led innovation across 7 countries and growing.
